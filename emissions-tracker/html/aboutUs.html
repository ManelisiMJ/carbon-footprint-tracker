ACRONYM

ACRONYM is one of the participating teams in the FinHub Hack Series I of 2023 . We are final-year students at the University of Cape Town (UCT) majoring in Computer Science.

This project aims to create awareness to the eminent issue of global warning. To achieve this goal, the project stores and reports data about the carbon emissions of a wide variety of companies.

The data is stored on the algorand blockchain. This means this data will be open to the public, secure and immutable.

Enhanced with the power of A.I, the carbon emissions tracker has the ability to draw predictions about a company's future carbon emissions.
